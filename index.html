About Me

As I grew older, I became more and more interested in software development, and I decided to pursue a career in this field. I am passionate about creating innovative solutions to complex problems, and I believe that software development is the perfect way to do this.

Throughout my time at the University of Arizona, I have had the opportunity to participate in an intensive software development bootcamp through the university's online program. This program has allowed me to gain valuable experience working on a number of real-world projects and has helped me develop a deeper understanding of software engineering principles.

During the bootcamp, I had the chance to work on projects in a variety of different programming languages and frameworks, including JavaScript, React, and Node.js. I also had the opportunity to collaborate with other aspiring software developers and industry professionals, which helped me gain valuable insights into the field.
